# 🧠 Psychology — What Interests You?

Before we design your personalised psychology course, answer the questions below!
For each one, note whether it sounds **very interesting**, **somewhat interesting**, or **not really my thing**.

---

## 1. Do you ever wonder *why* people do the things they do — even when it seems to make no sense?

> **Social Psychology** explores the hidden forces that drive human behaviour — why we conform, why we help strangers, and why ordinary people sometimes do extraordinary (or terrible) things.

**My interest level:** ___________________________

---

## 2. Are you fascinated by how the mind can both heal and harm the body?

> **Health Psychology** looks at the remarkable connection between your thoughts, emotions, and physical wellbeing — including why stress can literally make you sick, and how mindset can speed up recovery.

**My interest level:** ___________________________

---

## 3. Do you find yourself drawn to understanding people who've been through trauma or mental illness?

> **Abnormal & Clinical Psychology** dives into the full spectrum of the human mind — from anxiety and depression to rarer conditions — and how therapists help people rebuild their lives.

**My interest level:** ___________________________

---

## 4. Are you curious about how we become who we are — from babies to old age?

> **Developmental Psychology** follows the human journey from birth to death, exploring how we learn to love, think, form identity, and find meaning as we grow.

**My interest level:** ___________________________

---

## 5. Do you ever catch yourself reading people — their body language, expressions, or motivations?

> **Personality Psychology** asks the big question: what makes each of us uniquely *us*? It covers personality types, traits, and why two people in the same situation can react so differently.

**My interest level:** ___________________________

---

## 6. Have you ever been puzzled by a memory — why you remember some things vividly and forget others completely?

> **Cognitive Psychology** is like lifting the bonnet on the brain — exploring memory, attention, decision-making, and the surprising ways our thinking goes wrong.

**My interest level:** ___________________________

---

## 7. Are you interested in relationships — why some people connect deeply and others clash?

> **Relationship & Attachment Psychology** uncovers why we bond the way we do, how childhood shapes our adult relationships, and what makes love last.

**My interest level:** ___________________________

---

## 8. Do you care about how children learn and grow — or perhaps how people learn at any age?

> **Educational Psychology** explores how we absorb knowledge, what motivates us to keep going, and why some traditional teaching methods might actually be holding us back.

**My interest level:** ___________________________

---

## 9. Are you drawn to questions of happiness, purpose, and living a good life?

> **Positive Psychology** is the science of flourishing — studying resilience, gratitude, meaning, and what actually makes people happy (hint: it's not what most people think).

**My interest level:** ___________________________

---

## 10. Do you find yourself fascinated by crime, deception, or what drives people to extremes?

> **Forensic & Criminal Psychology** explores the psychology behind criminal behaviour, how investigators profile suspects, and what goes on in the mind of someone who breaks society's rules.

**My interest level:** ___________________________
